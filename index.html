Welcome

I am a PhD candidate in the Resource Economics Department at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. My primary research interests are industrial organization and applied microeconomics, with emphasis on policy evaluation and technological innovation. In addition to my economics training, I have extensive knowledge and experience in data science and computer programming. Further, I have extensive working experience in management positions coordinating multidisciplinary teams and developing public policy in the telecommunications and energy industries. I hold a M.S. in Resource Economics from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, a M.S. in Telecommunications Management from Oklahoma State University and a B.S. in Electronics Engineering from ESPE, Ecuador. Also, I completed a certification in Statistical and Computational Data Science in the College of Information and Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst.
